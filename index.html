About

Niloofar Azizi is a research scientist specializing in computer vision and machine learning, specifically focusing on graph neural networks. She is pursuing her PhD at Graz University of Technology under the supervision of Prof. Horst Bischof (2020-2024). Before her PhD, Niloofar completed her Master's degree at the University of Bonn (2015-2018, Prof. Sven Behnke) and a Bachelor's degree from Shiraz University (2009-2013, Prof. Reza Boostani).
